Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

What information we collect

When you book a consultation or download our patient guide, we collect your name and mobile number. We do not collect email addresses, payment information, or any other personal data through our website forms.

How we use your information

  • To confirm your appointment booking via WhatsApp
  • To send you the patient guide you requested
  • To contact you about your consultation

We do not use your information for unsolicited marketing. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

Data storage

Your information is stored securely in our clinic management system. We use Supabase (PostgreSQL) for data storage, with row-level security policies that restrict access to authorised staff only.

Analytics and tracking

We use Google Analytics 4 and Google Tag Manager to understand how visitors use our website. This data is aggregated and anonymous — we cannot identify individual visitors from analytics data. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

Your rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you. To make a request, contact us at info@drrajeshmalhotra.com or call 080-4567-8900.
